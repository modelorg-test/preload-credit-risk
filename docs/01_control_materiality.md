# Document Control & Materiality

## Classification

This model is classified as **Tier 1** under the CBUAE Model Risk Management framework due to its direct impact on capital adequacy calculations and provisioning decisions. Any material change to the model requires prior approval from the Model Risk Committee (MRC).

## Materiality Assessment

| Criterion | Rating | Justification |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Capital Impact | High | Directly feeds RWA calculations under Basel III |
| Revenue Sensitivity | Medium | Influences pricing decisions for personal loans |
| Regulatory Scrutiny | High | Subject to CBUAE quarterly review |
| Customer Impact | High | Determines loan approval/rejection for retail applicants |

## Document Governance

- **Review Frequency:** Quarterly (or upon material change)
- **Approval Authority:** Model Risk Committee
- **Document Owner:** Head of Retail Risk Modelling
- **Last Reviewed:** 2025-Q4
- **Next Review:** 2026-Q1
