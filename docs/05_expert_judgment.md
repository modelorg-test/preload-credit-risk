# Expert Judgement & Impact

## Qualitative Overlays

The Model Risk Committee approved the following manual adjustments during the Q1 2024 calibration review:

### Scorecard Cutoff Adjustments

Scorecard cutoffs were adjusted downward by **5%** for high-risk expatriate segments in response to macroeconomic volatility observed in Q4 2023 and early indicators of a softening labour market.

| Segment | Original Cutoff | Adjusted Cutoff | Rationale |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| UAE Nationals | 620 | 620 | No change — stable employment |
| GCC Expatriates | 640 | 608 | 5% reduction — sector diversification risk |
| Non-GCC Expatriates | 660 | 627 | 5% reduction — visa/employment volatility |

### Impact Assessment

The adjustment is expected to:

- Reduce approval rates for the affected segments by approximately **3.2%**
- Decrease expected portfolio loss by **AED 12M** annually
- Have negligible impact on overall portfolio Gini coefficient (< 0.5pp)

## Override Policy

All manual overrides of the scorecard decision must be:

1. Documented with a written rationale
2. Approved by a Senior Credit Officer (SCO) with delegated authority
3. Logged in the Model Override Register for quarterly MRC review
